# AgentGuard

> **Enterprise AI Operations Platform for Observability, Security, AI Quality, Governance, and Cost Management.**

AgentGuard is a unified platform designed to help organizations build, monitor, secure, evaluate, and govern AI applications throughout their entire lifecycle. It provides engineers, platform teams, and AI administrators with a single workspace to investigate AI requests, enforce guardrails, monitor performance, optimize costs, and ensure responsible AI usage.

---

## Vision

Modern AI applications require more than traditional monitoring.

AgentGuard brings together AI Observability, Security, Evaluation, Governance, and Cost Analytics into a single platform, enabling teams to confidently operate production AI systems.

Our goal is simple:

> **Build trustworthy AI by making every AI request observable, secure, measurable, and governable.**

# Platform Principles

AgentGuard is built around five core principles.

### Investigation First

Every workflow is designed to answer:

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- What should I do next?

---

### AI-Centric

Designed specifically for AI systems rather than traditional infrastructure monitoring.

---

### Progressive Disclosure

Start with summaries and insights, then progressively reveal deeper technical details only when needed.

---

### Unified Experience

Observability, Security, AI Quality, Governance, and Cost Management work together seamlessly within a consistent user experience.

---

### Enterprise Ready

Designed for production environments with scalability, security, accessibility, and extensibility in mind.
