# AgentGuard Theme Rationale

## Design Goal

AgentGuard is an Enterprise AI Operations Platform designed for **Observability, Security, AI Quality, Governance, and Administration**. The visual theme reflects **trust, intelligence, and operational excellence** while maintaining a calm enterprise experience.

---

## Primary Color — Teal

**Color:** `#14B8A6`

### Represents

- AI Intelligence
- Innovation
- Trust
- Operational Insights
- Modern Technology

### Why Teal?

Teal combines the reliability of blue with the intelligence and growth associated with green. It gives AgentGuard a modern AI-focused identity while remaining calm and professional for enterprise users.

### Used For

- Brand identity
- Primary buttons
- Active navigation
- Selected tabs
- Charts and analytics
- Interactive elements
- Focus states

---

## Secondary Color — Deep Navy

**Color:** `#1E3A5F`

### Represents

- Enterprise Reliability
- Stability
- Security
- Technical Confidence

### Why Deep Navy?

Deep Navy provides a strong enterprise foundation that complements Teal. It reinforces trust and professionalism without competing with the primary brand color.

### Used For

- Headers
- Navigation background
- Page titles
- Important labels
- Supporting UI elements

---

## Semantic Colors

| Color | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| 🟢 `#22C55E` | Success, Healthy Systems, Passed Evaluations |
| 🟠 `#F59E0B` | Warnings, Performance Issues, Recommendations |
| 🔴 `#EF4444` | Errors, Security Incidents, Failed Operations |
| ⚪ `#F8FAFC` | Workspace Background |
| ⚫ `#0D161C` | Dark Theme Background |

---

## Color Mapping to AgentGuard Modules

| Module | Color Meaning |
|---------|---------------|
| **Observability** | Teal highlights live insights and investigations |
| **Security** | Navy builds trust, while Red and Amber communicate risks |
| **AI Quality** | Teal represents intelligent evaluation, Green indicates successful outcomes |
| **Governance** | Navy reflects compliance, control, and reliability |
| **Administration** | Navy conveys enterprise management and stability |
| **Cost Management** | Teal emphasizes analytics and optimization insights |

---

## Design Philosophy

- **Teal** is the primary identity of AgentGuard, representing **AI intelligence, innovation, and operational insights**.
- **Deep Navy** provides a stable enterprise foundation, reinforcing **trust, reliability, and professionalism**.
- **Semantic colors** are reserved exclusively for system status, ensuring alerts, warnings, and health indicators remain immediately recognizable.

This combination creates a modern, trustworthy, and scalable visual identity that clearly differentiates AgentGuard as an **Enterprise AI Operations Platform**.
